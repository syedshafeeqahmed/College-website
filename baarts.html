Requirements

  • Before filling online application candidate must keep ready all the information required to fill the online form along with scanned copy of following documents.
  • Recent Photograph.
  • S.S.L.C Marks Card.
  • PUC TC/study/Bonafide certificate.
  • Aadhar card.
  • Pass Book.
  • Caste & Income certificate and signature.

How to Apply

  • Carefully fill and select all the details in the appropriate fields of online application form.
  • Ensure the Name of Candidate, the name of Father and name of mother exactly is as in SSLC or equivalent certificate/Marks Card.
  • Provide valid Mobile number and Email ID, it will be used for all further communication from the College.
  • Fees shall be paid online only. After payment of fee, candidate shall take the printout of application form and submit the same to college along with original documents and 1set Xerox copy of all documents on or before 31/08/2020.In case candidate fails to submit the hard copy of the application along with all original documents within stipulated date his/her admission stands cancelled without any intimation.
  • Candidates applying for Third and Fifth semester should submit the printed copy of application along with 1 set Xerox copy of caste & income certificate.

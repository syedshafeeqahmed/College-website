ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

The college is situated in the heart of Gulbarga city with a campus area of 11.03 acres. The college has multistoried building with built up area of around 85000 sq.ft. It has adequate number of classrooms, laboratories, library, administrative building and sports ground. The academic performance of students is observed to be very high obtaining a number of ranks consistently in the university examination. The college has active NCC, NSS, Red Cross and Scouts & Guides cells which have been organizing various events throughout the academic calendar.

Assessing the institution on several parameters of quality NAAC has re-accredited Government College Gulbarga with ‘A’ Grade in 2012.It is the only Government College in the entire Hyderabad Karnataka region which has been re-accredited with “A” grade by NAAC. It is the Fifth government college in the entire state to achieve such feat. The Government College, Kalaburagi has now acquired Autonomous Status granted by UGC from 2016-17.

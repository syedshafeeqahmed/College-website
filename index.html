About Us

Government College Kalaburagi is one of the oldest and leading institutions of higher learning in the educationally backward region of Hyderabad Karnataka region. The Government College, Gulbarga was stared in the year 1932 as an Intermediate College affiliated to Osmania University, Hyderabad in the erstwhile Nizam Government .Later with formation of new state of Karnataka in the year 1952 it was upgraded to a Degree college affiliated to Karnataka University, Dharwad. In the beginning B.A. and B.Sc. program was offered in various disciplines including languages. In the year 1980, when Gulbarga University was established in Kalaburagi, the college got affiliated to it. In the subsequent years i.e 1986, 2001, 2006 many UG courses such as B.Com., B.Sc. (Computer Science), and B.B.M. were started in the college. Thus, Government College, Kalaburagi became one among the few colleges in the Hyderabad Karnataka Region to offer UG courses in various faculties. The college added another cap to its feather, when Gulbarga University recognized it as PG centre and allowed to start PG courses in History and Economics in the year 2007. In the subsequent years many PG courses were started and at present 09 PG courses in various disciplines including Commerce, Mathematics and Computer Science are offered in the college. Present strength of the college is about 3000 students.
